CODE OF CONDUCT

We, the participants, come together for the open exchange of ideas, the freedom of thought, and expression. This requires an environment that recognizes and respects the inherent worth of every person. All participants, are required to agree with this Code of Conduct. All participants must commit themselves to providing an experience for all that is:

- FREE FROM harassment, bullying, and discrimination which includes but is not limited to: Offensive comments related to age, race, religion, creed, color, gender (including transgender/gender identity/gender expression), sexual orientation, medical condition, physical or intellectual disability, pregnancy, or medical conditions, national origin or ancestry.

- FREE FROM intimidation, personal attacks, harassment, unnecessary disruption. Inappropriate behavior that interferes with another's full participation including: sexual harassment, stalking, following, harassing photography or recording, inappropriate physical contact, unwelcome attention, public vulgar exchanges.

- FREE FROM use of images, activities, or other materials that are of a sexual, racial, or otherwise offensive nature that may create an inappropriate or toxic environment. Free from disruptive conduct including fighting, coercion, theft, damage to property.

If a participant engages in any inappropriate behavior as defined above, they may receive a formal or informal warning or a ban. All participants are encouraged to report violations of this policy to the Strawberry Pancakes administrators.
